Rules Of Origin Trade Solutions (ROOTS) is a service specialized in rules of origin determination, validation, and certification of origin, as well as origin-related procedures.

ROOTS provides all necessary tools for a reliable, comprehensive and robust management of rules of origin within your company, and with your suppliers and customers.

Main Pillars

Intelligent system

Swiftly calculate the origin for multiple Agreements.

Optimize costs and time when evaluating different supply alternatives.

Virtual file

Easily control, access, monitor and manage all your origin files.

Information available in real time for suppliers and customers.

Consulting

Permanent expert advice.

Consultancy and accompaniment in origin audits

Intelligent system

Perform the origin calculations in seconds with three data upload options:

  • Upload data via an XLS/CSV file, or
  • Capture data manually

Allow for evaluation if a product qualifies as originating for multiple Agreements simultaneously, and help to identify areas of opportunity for alternative supply sources.

Generate validation filters for negotiated goods, for each Free Trade Agreement and at the country level.

Compare:

  • Versions of the same product (prototype), based on different suppliers
  • The same product, under the rules of origin of different Agreements

Issue alerts on:

  • Changes in rules of origin
  • Validity of certificates

Generate

  • Certification of origin (self-certification)
  • File to process RPE in VUCEM

Virtual file

Facilitate storage, access and management of files that support the compliance of origin of goods in one place.

Organize the files in a structured way according to your needs.

Integrate the file in real time with all participants (company, suppliers and customers).

Allow to have permanently swift access to the files related to the origin of the goods.

Identify in an agile and simple way the lack of information, allowing complete traceability.

Maintain different versions of documents that are prepared, reviewed and validated.

Shield from modifications to the final file used to generate the certification.

Store the information for at least the period required by the authority.

Consulting

Horizontal consultations for all services, offering the user:

  • Training for the use of the system.
  • Consultancy on the interpretation and application of the rules of origin of the different Free Trade Agreements.
  • Accompaniment in:
    • The entire process of origin determination.
    • Preparation of files.
    • Preventive and performance audits.
    • Information requirements by the authority.
