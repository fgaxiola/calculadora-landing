About ROOTS

ROOTS (Rules Of Origin Trade Solutions) is a specialized service dedicated to simplifying and optimizing the complex world of rules of origin determination, validation, and certification for businesses engaged in international trade.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, comprehensive, and robust tools that enable companies to efficiently manage rules of origin within their organization and seamlessly collaborate with suppliers and customers. We believe that origin compliance should be straightforward, transparent, and accessible to all businesses, regardless of their size.

What We Do

ROOTS offers a complete suite of services designed to help companies navigate the complexities of rules of origin:

Intelligent Origin Calculation

Our advanced system allows you to calculate origin for multiple Free Trade Agreements simultaneously, saving time and reducing errors. With three flexible data upload options—direct ERP/CRM integration, file upload (XLS/CSV), or manual entry—you can evaluate supply alternatives and optimize costs efficiently.

Virtual File Management

Centralize all your origin-related documentation in one secure, accessible location. Our virtual file system enables real-time collaboration with suppliers and customers, ensuring complete traceability and compliance with regulatory requirements.

Expert Consulting

Our team of origin experts provides ongoing support, training, and guidance throughout your origin determination process. From system implementation to audit preparation, we're here to help you succeed.

Why Choose ROOTS?

  • Time Savings: Determine origin for multiple agreements simultaneously
  • Origin by Design: Plan origin before manufacturing products
  • Intelligent Integration: Direct communication with your company's ERP
  • Certainty: Assure customers and buyers about origin compliance
  • Strategic Support: Access to expert advice when you need it
  • Complete Documentation: All origin files in one place

Our Technology

ROOTS is built on cutting-edge AWS Cloud Services, ensuring optimal performance, security, and scalability. Our Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) model means you can access your data anywhere, anytime, with guaranteed security through our encryption processes.

Commitment to Excellence

We are committed to keeping our origin rules database updated with the latest market changes, ensuring your calculations are always accurate and aligned with current regulations. Our modular approach allows us to continuously add new features based on client feedback and industry needs.
